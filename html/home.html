What is LeWonder?

LeWonder is an organisation that thrives to develop interest in science and wonderment,
through content creation. It was found by Dr.GDR on 10 Nov 2018, celebrating the International Science Day.
This recently born organisation has reached over millons of it's followers within 4 years, with 20 working people.
